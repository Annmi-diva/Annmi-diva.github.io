Cozumel Whale Shark Tours

Swim with the world's largest fish and cross it off your bucket list. After this experience you'll never look at life the same way again.
This incredible tour is available for a short season only, while whale sharks are around the tip of the Yucatan peninsula about 70 miles (110km) from Cozumel.
The whale shark season is expected to start on May 28, 2022 and end on September 17, 2022.

Cozumel Sailing Tour

Simply the best sail and snorkel tour to the remote north coast of Cozumel.
Spend an amazing 6 hours of your vacation on this wonderful 35 ft trimaran trip.
Combine sailing, snorkeling, lunch, drinks, great music and beautiful coastline on this unforgettable experience.
The boat is like a catamaran but with 3 hulls instead of 2, making it extra stable in the water. The crew is experienced and work excellently as a team, making sure you have the best experience possible.
They can take up to 28 people, but groups are limited to 16 or 17 so you have plenty of room and can really relax without feeling crowded.

Private Chichen Itza Tour By Land

This is a Pandemic-Buster tour with outdoor stops and a vehicle just for you, so it limits interaction with other tourists you don't know.
The incredible Mayan ruins at Chichen Itza are one of the Seven New Wonders of the World and a visit should be on everyone's bucket-list.
With our private VIP tour by land you'll travel comfortably in a van just for your group and will take the toll highway which is much smoother and faster.
Once there, your private guide will show you round the site, including the famous El Castillo pyramid and El Caracol observatory. You'll be in awe of the majestic structures and surrounding jungle.
